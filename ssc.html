Get ready for the SSC'20 on 11th - 13th December,2020.

SSC 20

The Congress is an annual flagship event of IEEE Hyderabad Section, which is an amalgamation of technical talks, sessions on soft skills, and career opportunities, wherein student members of various Universities/ Colleges under the IEEE Hyderabad Section meet to learn, discuss and network. Last year SSC was organized for 1 days with more than 1100 delegates from all the Student Branches and 50+ volunteers. The Student Congress aims to congregate student members of the IEEE Student Activities Committee to enable them to discuss the growth and development of IEEE student activities not only members sharing their experiences with student members. Section Student Congress is being held on 12th – 13th December 2020 with the theme of “Use of Digital Technology during pandemic situation”. This year we are going to keep the delegates engrossed in a plethora of professional talks, competitions, ice breaking activities that brings out enthusiast in delegates attending the congress With some of the sharpest minds and illustrious dignitaries of the nation converging under one roof, and exchanging their variable insights, one can carve out their own niche and work in synergy.
